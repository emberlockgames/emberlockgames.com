"SSS"

It is 2805.

Humanity has recovered from its darkest times, colonized the solar system with the aid of artificial intelligence, and now expands into the stars. In the wake of the edge of progress, desperate people and broken machines are left behind, to make of themselves what they can...

You are a Scavenger.

Designed as a traditional real-world board game, a game of SSS is a quick progression in-and-back-out-of a derelict spacecraft, battling continous opposition.

Retrieve the ship's Treasure from the wreck's depths and get it back to your boarding craft at all costs. Your squad is expendable, that Treasure is not!

SSS can be played alone, or co-operatively with a squad of other players. For the alpha release, all games (even single-player) will have to be run remotely on an official game server. Beta releases and beyond will include a stand-alone server for single-player and LAN games (or your own public hosting).
